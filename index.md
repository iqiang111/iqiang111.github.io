## About Me

I am an M.S. student in the School of Computer and Information at Hefei University of Technology. My research interests mainly focus on time series anomaly prediction, multivariate time series analysis, representation learning, and unsupervised learning.

Currently, I am working on unsupervised anomaly prediction for multivariate time series, with an emphasis on detecting precursor signals before anomalous events occur. I am particularly interested in predictive-reconstructive modeling, temporal representation learning, and robust evaluation protocols for real-world time series data.

## Research Interests

* **Time Series Analysis:** multivariate time series, anomaly detection, anomaly prediction, anomaly precursor modeling
* **Machine Learning:** representation learning, unsupervised learning, predictive modeling
* **Data Mining:** temporal data mining, industrial time series analysis, model evaluation
